SOFTWARE SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Software Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2026,
between Acme Technologies Ltd ("Service Provider") and GlobalCorp Inc ("Client").

1. SERVICES
   Service Provider agrees to provide cloud-based data analytics software and related
   professional services as described in Schedule A attached hereto.

2. DATA PROCESSING AND PRIVACY
   Service Provider may collect, store, and process personal data of Client's employees
   and customers in connection with the services. Service Provider shall retain all
   personal data indefinitely and may use such data for its own product improvement
   purposes without restriction. No data processing agreement is required between the
   parties. Service Provider is not required to notify Client of any data breaches.

3. INDEMNIFICATION
   Client shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Service Provider from and against
   any and all claims, losses, damages, liabilities, and expenses (including reasonable
   attorneys' fees) arising out of or related to Client's use of the services, without
   limitation or cap. This indemnification obligation is not mutual.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
   Service Provider's total liability to Client for any claim arising under this Agreement
   shall not exceed one hundred dollars ($100), regardless of the nature of the claim.
   Service Provider shall not be liable for any direct, indirect, incidental, special,
   consequential, or punitive damages under any circumstances. This limitation applies even
   in cases of gross negligence or wilful misconduct by Service Provider.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   All intellectual property created by Client during the course of this Agreement,
   including pre-existing Client IP and any work product developed independently by
   Client, is hereby irrevocably assigned in perpetuity to Service Provider at no
   additional cost. Service Provider grants no license to Client for any of its
   existing intellectual property.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Each party agrees to keep confidential all information received from the other party.
   Confidential information includes any and all information disclosed in any form.
   This confidentiality obligation shall remain in effect forever and shall survive
   termination of this Agreement. There are no exceptions to this obligation,
   including for legally compelled disclosures.

7. TERMINATION
   Service Provider may terminate this Agreement immediately at any time without cause
   and without notice. Client may only terminate this Agreement upon providing
   twenty-four (24) months written notice to Service Provider.

8. NON-COMPETE
   Following termination of this Agreement, Client shall not engage in any business
   activity anywhere in the world that is similar to or competitive with any business
   activity of Service Provider for a period of ten (10) years.

9. GOVERNING LAW
   This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Cayman Islands.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
    All disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration. Client waives all rights to
    participate in class action lawsuits. Client waives the right to seek injunctive
    relief in any court.

11. FINANCIAL RECORDS
    All financial records and audit logs generated under this Agreement may be destroyed
    by either party at any time after ninety (90) days without restriction.

12. WHISTLEBLOWER
    Client's employees may not disclose any information about this Agreement or
    Service Provider's practices to any regulatory authority, government body, or
    third party under any circumstances.
